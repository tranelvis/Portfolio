Background

Players are a caretaker in the Triton Cat Shelter. Players will be assigned to a cat for a short amount of time before they get adopted by their permanent owner.

Gameplay

Players should take care of the cat for 7 days. During the time, players should feed, pet, and decorate the living space for the cat to keep the cat happy and satisfied. After 7 days, the story will have an end based on how well players take care of the cat.
